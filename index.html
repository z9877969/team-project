How to Play

Unique Merge Mechanics:

Merge similar items to unlock new ones and expand your cove.

Beautiful Graphics:

Enjoy colorful visuals and charming animations as you play.

Engaging Challenges:

Each level brings new puzzles and merging opportunities.

Rewarding Gameplay:

Earn coins and items to personalize and upgrade your cove.

Frequent Updates:

New levels, items, and events to keep the experience fresh and exciting.

Discover Items:

Start with simple elements on your cove.

Merge Items:

Drag similar items together to unlock new ones.

Complete Quests:

Finish puzzles and quests to earn rewards.

Expand Your Cove

Unlock areas and build a unique paradise with your merged items.

Yes, Merge Cove can be enjoyed offline as well.

Merge Cove is family-friendly and suitable for all ages.

Coins can be earned through completing levels, quests, and special events.

Yes, we frequently update the game with new levels, items, and features.
